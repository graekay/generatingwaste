Quest Generator

Setting Faction Disposition

Set factions disposition towards you by clicking the radio buttons. Friendly, Neutral, Hostile.
Not setting disposition exludes the faction from the generator.

Quest Type

From the dropdown selector at the bottom you can select from 4 quest types.

Retieve

A Friendly or Neutral faction wants you to get an item from a specified location.
Generated Items are narrative and don't serve any other purpose.

Rescue

A Friendly or Neutral faction wants you to rescue a member of their faction
from a Neutral or Hostile faction.

Escort

A Friendly or Neutral faction wants you to go with one of their members from location to location
through a Neutral or Hostile factions territory.

Eliminate

A Friendly or Neutral faction wants you to kill a member of a Neutral or Hostile Faction.
At a specified location.
